Concept
How do you check your iPhone Application's sales?
This is a viewer of your AppStore sales info.
By "StoreSales", you can send sales info to iPhone and visualize them your AppStore sales info using "StoreSales for MacOSX". "StoreSales for MacOSX" is an application on MacOSX. You can download sales logs from iTunes connect with it.
You can check sales info with daily, weekly, every country and every app.
And, in landscape mode, sales infomation such as daily, weekly is drawn as line and circle chart.
Check your application sales with your iPhone/iPod touch!
